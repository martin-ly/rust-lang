### 1. 理论深度 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**形式化分析**:

```text
Actor 系统语义:
  Config = (A, M)
  转换规则:
    actor a ∈ A, mailbox(a) = [m|ms],
    β(state(a), m) = (s', actions)
    ⟹ (A, M) → (A', M')

不变量:
  ∀ a ∈ Actors: state(a) is private
  消息有序性: msg₁ before msg₂ ⟹ 处理(msg₁) before 处理(msg₂)
```
